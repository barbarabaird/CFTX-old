Elements will help newcomers better understand the philosophy and methodology of CrossFit as Well as the 9 functional movements (Air Squats, Front Squat, Overhead Squat, Shoulder Press, Push Press, Push Jerk, Deadlift, Sumo Deadlift High Pull, and Medicine Ball Clean).
Elements includes: 3- 1 hour sessions with a CrossFit Texas coach, measurements and body fat analysis, nutrition 101, and goal setting. This can be done in 1- 3 hour session on a Saturday or a Sunday.
Start times are flexible. Email info@crossfittx.com to set up your session.

Elements must be taken by all beginner crossfitters.

CrossFit Varsity is a strength and conditioning program for teens that include functional movements that build endurance, coordination, flexibility, agility and balance. Our class time is broken into a group warm-up, skill/strength, WOD, and cool down stretch. We will have a nutrition lesson every week. The program is scalable for any age or experience level.

Ages:11-16 yrs. old

Varsity is T/Th 6:30pm Coached by Blyth


Designed by NFL players and some of the top coaches in the world from varying sports, Power Athlete is a comprehensive strength and conditioning program designed for participants in sports requiring speed, strength, and capacity. The Power Athlete program is designed to work for all players regardless of age or experience. The loads, distances, times, intensity, and programming can be scaled, and the program has been designed to meet the needs of athletes at all levels of training advancement.

Ages:8th grade athletes working towards high school sports and 9th grade+
